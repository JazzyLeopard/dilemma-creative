## Key Takeaway

**Nobody owns "food + beauty social media in Flanders" in search.** This is a clear positioning gap. By creating niche-specific content in Dutch (nl-BE) targeting both food and beauty entrepreneurs, Dilemma Creative can rank for keywords no competitor is actively pursuing.

### Biggest Opportunities
1. **Dutch blog content** about social media for food & beauty — almost no competition
2. **Niche-specific case studies** — most competitors don't publish detailed results
3. **"Social media bureau food Vlaanderen"** — virtually uncontested keyword
4. **Educational resources** — templates, guides, checklists for food/beauty entrepreneurs
5. **Local cultural angle** — Flemish voice and understanding vs. NL-based competitors
