**Industry:** Social Media Management & Content Creation Agency

## Brand Story

Dilemma Creative is born from a simple truth: small business owners in the food and beauty industry are passionate about their craft, but overwhelmed by social media. They face a daily dilemma — focus on what they love, or spend hours creating content they're unsure about.

Dilemma Creative is the fix. We grab you by the hand and guide you through the social media maze with energy, clarity, and creativity. No jargon, no confusion — just results that make your brand shine online.

**NL:** Dilemma Creative is geboren uit een simpele waarheid: kleine ondernemers in de food & beauty sector zijn gepassioneerd over hun vak, maar overweldigd door social media. Ze staan dagelijks voor een dilemma — focussen op wat ze liefhebben, of uren besteden aan content waar ze onzeker over zijn.

Dilemma Creative is de oplossing. Wij nemen je bij de hand en gidsen je door het social media doolhof met energie, duidelijkheid en creativiteit. Geen jargon, geen verwarring — alleen resultaten die jouw merk online laten stralen.

## Logo

### Concept Direction

The Dilemma Creative logo plays on the **question mark** as the central brand symbol — representing the "dilemma" that clients face. The question mark is reimagined as a dynamic, playful element:

- **Wordmark:** "dilemma" in Space Grotesk Bold, lowercase
- **Icon:** A stylized question mark (❓) that doubles as a creative spark/lightbulb
- **The dot** of the question mark can be replaced with a colorful gradient circle
- **"creative"** sits below or beside in DM Sans, lighter weight

### Logo Variations

1. **Full lockup** — icon + wordmark (horizontal)
2. **Stacked** — icon on top, wordmark below
3. **Icon only** — question mark symbol (for favicons, profile pics)
4. **Wordmark only** — text without icon

### Clear Space

Minimum clear space = height of the "d" in "dilemma" on all sides.

### Minimum Sizes

- Full lockup: min 120px wide
- Icon only: min 32px
- Wordmark: min 80px wide

### Color Usage

- **On dark:** White wordmark + gradient icon
- **On light:** Deep Night wordmark + gradient icon
- **Monochrome:** All white OR all Deep Night
- **Never:** Don't stretch, rotate, change colors of individual letters, add shadows

## Tone of Voice

### Personality in Writing

| Do                                        | Don't                                     |
|-------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------|
| Sound like a knowledgeable friend         | Sound corporate or stiff                  |
| Use active, energetic language            | Use passive, complicated sentences        |
| Be direct and clear                       | Use industry jargon without explanation   |
| Show enthusiasm with purpose              | Overuse exclamation marks!!!!             |
| Use "we" and "you" (personal)             | Use "the agency" or "one" (impersonal)   |

## Photography & Imagery Direction

### Style

- **Bright & airy** — natural lighting, vibrant colors
- **Authentic** — real moments, not overly staged
- **Close-ups** — textures of food, beauty products, hands at work
- **Behind-the-scenes** — showing the process, the real story
- **Colorful backdrops** — using brand colors as props or backgrounds

### Food Industry

- Overhead flat-lays with colorful ingredients
- Steam, texture, drizzle — make it sensory
- Hands preparing, plating, serving
- Restaurant ambiance with warm tones

### Beauty Industry

- Clean product shots with bold color accents
- Before/after with tasteful presentation
- Close-up textures (creams, powders, liquids)
- Soft lighting with pops of brand color

### Image Treatment

- Light grain overlay (2-4%) for warmth
- Optional subtle gradient overlay using brand colors at 10-15% opacity
- Round corners on social media cards (16px radius)
- Drop shadows: soft, colored (brand tint at 10% opacity)

## Social Media Templates

### Instagram Grid Strategy

- **Pattern:** Alternate between content types (tip, portfolio, testimonial, behind-the-scenes)
- **Color coding:** Each content type has a dominant brand color
- **Consistency:** Always use brand fonts and at least one brand color per post

### Template Types

1. **Quote/Tip cards** — Gradient background + white text
2. **Portfolio showcases** — Image + brand-colored border/frame
3. **Carousel educationals** — Consistent slide design with numbered pages
4. **Story templates** — Poll stickers, Q&A with brand colors
5. **Reels covers** — Bold text overlay, brand gradient bottom bar

### Grid Aesthetic

The grid should feel like a **colorful mosaic** — each post distinct but clearly part of the same family through consistent typography, color accents, and composition style.

## Component Styles

### Buttons

- **Primary:** Coral Crush background, white text, 12px radius, bold
- **Secondary:** Electric Grape outline, 12px radius
- **Ghost:** Transparent, brand color text, underline on hover
- **Hover:** Slight scale (1.02) + shadow increase
- **Active:** Scale down (0.98)

### Cards

- White or Cloud background
- 16px border radius
- Subtle shadow: `0 4px 24px rgba(26, 26, 46, 0.06)`
- Hover: lift with increased shadow

### Tags/Badges

- Pill shape (full radius)
- Brand color at 10% opacity background
- Brand color text
- 12px font, medium weight

## Motion Guidelines

### Principles

- **Purposeful:** Every animation communicates something
- **Quick:** 200-400ms for UI elements
- **Smooth:** Use ease-out for entrances, ease-in for exits
- **Subtle:** Enhance, never distract

### Easing

- **Default:** `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)` — smooth, organic
- **Bounce:** `cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)` — playful emphasis
- **Linear:** For continuous effects (loading, rotation)

### Common Animations

- **Fade in up:** opacity 0 -> 1, translateY 20px -> 0 (400ms)
- **Scale in:** scale 0.95 -> 1, opacity 0 -> 1 (300ms)
- **Slide in:** translateX -20px -> 0, opacity 0 -> 1 (350ms)
