# SEO Strategy — Dilemma Creative

> Social media beheer & content creatie voor food & beauty ondernemers in Vlaanderen.

## Executive Summary

Dilemma Creative operates in a niche sweet spot: **food + beauty social media management in Flanders**. Most competitors are either generalist agencies or focus on only one vertical (e.g., Picant = horeca only, The Dot Society = fashion/luxury DTC). By owning the intersection of food & beauty content creation in Dutch-speaking Belgium, Dilemma Creative can dominate a specific, underserved keyword cluster.

**Primary goals**: Lead generation + brand awareness (equal weight)
**Location model**: Fully remote (no local SEO / Google Business Profile needed)
**Content capacity**: 1x per week
**Language**: Dutch (nl-BE), with potential English content later
**Target market**: Food entrepreneurs (restaurants, bakeries, food brands) and beauty entrepreneurs (salons, skincare brands, aesthetic clinics) in Flanders

## Competitive Positioning

### Unique Angle
No direct competitor in Flanders combines **food + beauty** as dual niches with a content-first approach. This is the positioning gap:

- **Picant**: Horeca only, West-Vlaanderen only, no blog/content marketing
- **Skillmedia**: Multi-sector generalist (beauty is one of 8+ verticals)
- **The Dot Society**: Fashion/luxury DTC, English-focused, performance marketing
- **Lekker Sociaal Media**: Netherlands-based, horeca only
- **De Horeca Marketeer**: Netherlands-based, horeca only

### Dilemma Creative's SEO Moat
1. **Niche authority**: Only agency positioning as food + beauty specialist in Flanders
2. **Dutch-first content**: Native nl-BE content (competitors are either NL-based or English-focused)
3. **Content-led SEO**: Weekly blog builds topical authority competitors aren't pursuing
4. **E-E-A-T signals**: Case studies with real results from food & beauty clients

### Authoritativeness
- Case studies with measurable results (follower growth, engagement, bookings)
- Client testimonials with names and businesses

### Trust
- Transparent pricing or pricing tiers on the site
- Clear process page showing what clients can expect
- Privacy policy, terms, and professional contact information

## Technical SEO Requirements

### Core Web Vitals Targets
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| LCP | < 2.5s |
| INP | < 200ms |
| CLS | < 0.1 |

### Technical Checklist
- [x] Next.js with SSR/SSG for fast page loads
- [ ] Implement structured data (Organization, ProfessionalService, Article)
- [ ] XML sitemap generation
- [ ] robots.txt configuration
- [ ] Canonical URLs on all pages
- [ ] Open Graph + Twitter Card meta on all pages
- [ ] Image optimization with next/image and WebP
- [ ] Proper heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
- [ ] Internal linking between related content
- [ ] 301 redirects strategy (if migrating from other domain)

### AI Search Readiness (GEO)
- [ ] Clear, quotable service descriptions for AI Overviews
- [ ] Structured FAQ content that AI can extract
- [ ] Organization schema with complete business information
- [ ] Allow GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot in robots.txt
- [ ] Consider llms.txt file for AI crawler guidance
